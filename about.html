About Fixleos

Our Mission

Fixleos was founded with the vision to simplify home services and bring trusted professionals to your doorstep. We aim to deliver prompt, reliable, and high-quality solutions for every household need.

Our Vision

We envision a world where booking a skilled technician is as easy as a click. By leveraging technology and rigorous quality checks, we strive to build the most transparent and customer-centric service platform in India.

Why Choose Fixleos?

  • Verified Experts: All professionals undergo strict KYC and background checks.
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden charges—know the cost upfront.
  • 24/7 Support: Our team is available round the clock to assist you.
  • Easy Booking: Just a few taps on your phone or a click on the website and you’re done.
