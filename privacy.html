Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fixleos. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect
  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address when you register an account or place a booking.
  • Service Details: Service category, sub-service, booking date/time, and address you provide during booking.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details, payment method (e.g., UPI, card), and payment status.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, device information, pages visited, and referral URLs to improve our website performance.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information.
3. How We Use Your Information
  • To Provide & Maintain Services: Process bookings, manage user and provider accounts, and send notifications.
  • To Communicate: Send you confirmations, reminders, updates, and support correspondence via email, SMS, or push notifications.
  • To Improve our Website: Analyze usage trends and tailor content, optimize user experience, and troubleshoot issues.
  • To Process Payments: Facilitate transactions with payment gateways and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • To Comply with Laws: Carry out legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
4. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your personal information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Third-party partners (e.g., payment gateways, SMS/Email providers) to complete services and send notifications.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety and that of our users.
5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to collect information and improve our services. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our website. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of our website may not function properly.

6. Security of Your Information

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, such as SSL encryption for data in transit and secure server storage. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights
  • Access & Correction: You may request a copy of your personal data or ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to applicable laws and our legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications by following unsubscribe instructions or contacting us.
8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We recommend you review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
